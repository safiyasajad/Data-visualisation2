Overall cases in Malaysia

Urbanized cities such as Kuala Lumpur, Selangor, and Johor exhibit higher crime rates.

Kuala Lumpur has the highest with 913 cases, followed closely by Selangor at 868.

Rapid urbanization, economic opportunities, and Johor’s role as a logistics hub near Singapore drive population growth, creating social challenges that increase criminal activity.

Types of Crime by Year

Between 2016 and 2022, Malaysia experienced an overall decline in major crimes.

Assaults fell from 5,531 to 3,807, homicides from 456 to 240, and burglary and theft rates also dropped significantly. Armed gang robberies declined sharply, while unarmed gang robberies decreased more gradually, reflecting improved public safety.
